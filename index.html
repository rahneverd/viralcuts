How many videos will I get for each plan?

For Part-Timer you'll get up to a maximum of 6 videos per month. For Creator, typically customers see an average of 20 videos per month, but this can be more or less depending on the amount of Hi-Fi videos. The Studio effectively doubles the output of Creator, so roughly 40 videos per month.

What's the difference between Hi-Fi and Low-Fi?

Great question! The videos you see on our site are what like to call High-Fidelity and they can take up to 2-3 days to animate. Here's an example Hi-Fi video with millions of views. Lower-Fidelity videos typically take a few hours to animate. Here's an example of a Low-Fi video.

How long does a video take?

We think of videos in two ways: High-Fi and Low-Fi. High-Fi videos are like the ones you see on the site - they usually take about 48 hours. Low-fi videos are simple captions, b-roll, and light animations - we can make a few of those per day. We'll provide you with a timeline and keep you updated on the progress each step of the way.

Are you sure this will help me grow?

We don't guarantee growth, but yeah, it should help you grow. Our videos perform 10x the average - it all comes down to content though. For meaningful growth, you have to be creating content that connects with people. If you're unsure how to do that, our team can help!

Can you take my long-form videos and turn it into shorts?

Yes we can! You can send us long-form video content or even a YouTube link and we'll turn it into high-performing shorts. If you want to be as hands-off as possible, we recommend having one of our writers pick out the juiciest content.

Why wouldn’t I just hire my own animator?

You could but it's a pain in the ass. In our experience, it often doesn’t end well unless you have experience managing freelancers, which is a headache in itself. We remove all the risk by managing the process and quality checks for you.

Do you offer trials or make example videos?

No, we do not. If you're looking to try ViralCuts out, Part-Timer is your best option. You can cancel at anytime.

Can I cancel if I don’t like it?

There's no contracts or long-term agreements. You can pause or cancel at anytime. If we have dedicated an animators time and started work on your videos before you decide to cancel, you won't be eligible for a refund.

What if I'm not happy with my video?

This doesn't happen very often but if it does - you get unlimited revisions! We'll keep tweaking things until you absolutely love your videos.

Do you only make animated clips?

No, we offer a range of video editing services, including animated and live-action clips, promotional videos, corporate videos, and more. We can customize our services to meet your specific needs.

How do I submit my content?

Your account manager will onboard you to Asana, our project management tool, and you'll submit footage & feedback there. It's super simple.
